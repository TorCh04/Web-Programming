Volleyball Rotations for Dummies

This website is just for me to learn the standard 5-1 volleyball rotations

1st Rotation

  • This is when the Setter is in Position 1
  • Your primary passers are 4B & Libero/M1
  • Your secondary passers Opp & 4A
  • Overhand pass is allowed and welcomed more often to intimidate servers
  • This is often our first rotation and one we can side out quickly: 3 front row attackers and pipe through 6

6th Rotation

  • This is when the Setter is in Position 6
  • Your primary passers are 4B & Libero/M1 & 4A
  • Your secondary passers Opp & 4A
  • In this rotation 4B is normally the target for serve as trying to eliminate them from the attack
  • We can move 4B short, shift M1/L & 4A to left and drop M2 back to receive
  • Whilst the setter is back court in 5 be aware of your rotatio
  • Overhand pass is allowed and welcomed more often to intimidate servers
  • This is sometimes a sticky rotation as one of our front court attackers may be targetted: 3 front court attackers and pipe attack through 6
